## What is Open Credit?

**Definition:** Open Credit is what happens when credit markets move from closed, relationship-driven coordination to open, data-driven coordination — making credit faster, broader, and more investable.

### The Evolution of Credit
- **Credit 1.0 - Bank Credit:** Banks originated and distributed through public markets
- **Credit 2.0 - Private Credit:** Post-2008, non-bank credit flourished ($2.0T AUM), valued for flexibility but concentrated and analog
- **Credit 3.0 - Open Credit:** Technology removes friction between capital and borrowers. Credit flows at the speed and cost of the internet.

- Crypto has speed and technology but doesn't speak 'credit'

## Communication Style

### Do
- Cadence awareness — slow and pauses add credibility
- Reduce filler words
- Less is more; defer to simple language
- Ground claims in concrete proof
- Be audience-aware

### Don't
- Don't over-emphasize technology at expense of credit story
- Don't use jargon without translation
- Don't pitch platform ambitions too early
- Don't say "RWA" without context

### Tone
- Professional but not stuffy
- Confident but not arrogant
- Technical when needed, accessible always
